1. General Performance Questions
- What is the total sales for each branch over a specific period?
- How is the total sales distributed across product lines?
- What is the monthly average sales per branch?
2. Customer-Related Questions
- Is there a difference in sales between customer types (Normal vs. Member)?
- Are there product preferences based on customer gender (Male vs. Female)?
- Which customer category is most loyal (in terms of purchase frequency or average spending)?
3. Product and Product Line Questions
- What are the best-selling products by product line?
- What is the average unit price for each product line?
- How does the performance of product lines differ across branches or cities?
4. Branch and City Questions
- What is the performance of each branch in terms of sales and revenue?
- Which city records the highest average sales?
- How do payment methods differ across branches or cities?
5. Tax-Related Questions
- What is the total tax collected over a specific period?
- Is there a sales impact when taxes are applied?
6. Payment Method Questions
- What are the most commonly used payment methods by customers?
- Are there differences in total spending based on payment methods?
7. Customer Rating Questions
- What is the average customer rating per branch or product line?
- Is there a relationship between higher ratings and increased sales?
- Which products or branches receive the lowest ratings, and why?
8. Time and Date Questions
- What are the peak times (hour and day) for sales?
- How are sales distributed over the week, month, or year?
- Is there a seasonal effect (such as holidays or special events) on sales?
9. Profitability Questions
- What is the overall profit margin for each product line?
- What are the most profitable products?
- How do quantities sold and taxes affect overall profit?
10. Trend and Change Questions
- How have sales changed over time?
- Are there any specific trends over certain periods (e.g., increase in sales of a particular product)?
-Are there fluctuations in customer ratings or satisfaction over time?
